Guidelines and Agreement

We share the prayer requests in our Live sessions (Saturdays & Sundays). If you do not want your prayer request to be shared publicly, please state this fact while you submit your prayer request. Your identity will always remain confidential. Feel free to submit your prayer requests as ‘anonymous’.
We reserve the right to reject your submission, or to edit/delete any part of it that uses foul, insulting, or crude language, even include the right to ban violators from submitting again.
